# Project Governance

This document outlines the governance model for the **ADITYA OS** project.

## Maintainer Roles & Responsibilities

Maintainers are individuals who have demonstrated dedication, skill, and alignment with the project's vision. They have write access to the main codebase and are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests.
- Deciding project roadmap and release schedules.
- Triaging issues and providing support.
- Upholding and enforcing the [Code of Conduct](../.github/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Decision Making Process

Our goal is to reach consensus on all technical decisions, architecture, and feature integrations.

### Lazy Consensus

For small modifications, bug fixes, and routine tasks, we operate under a model of "Lazy Consensus". A maintainer proposes a change, and if there are no objections within 48 hours, it is assumed to have consensus.

### Active Consensus

For major features, architectural restructuring, API breakages, and structural additions, active approval from at least two maintainers is required before merging.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who show consistent, high-quality involvement over several months (e.g., submitting high-quality PRs, reviewing code, improving docs, participating in discussions) may be nominated by an existing maintainer to join the maintainers team.
